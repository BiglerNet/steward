# Apple Sign in with Apple

Official docs: -
https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/sign-in-with-apple -
https://developer.apple.com/documentation/signinwithapple/displaying-sign-in-with-apple-buttons-on-the-web

## Official assets

Apple provides official button generation and downloadable assets from
the docs above.

Use only: - Black button (light UI) - White button (dark UI) - White
with black outline - Icon-only variant

Do not recreate the Apple logo or alter colors.

Recommended implementation: - Light mode: black button - Dark mode:
white button
